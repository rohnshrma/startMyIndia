About Us

Start My India is an Accelerator and Incubator program for upcoming and running Start-ups and growing companies. We provide them access to mentorship, investments, and various services to our budding visionaries.
In a world where people are not afraid to fulfill their dreams of becoming independent and owning their business, we provide them with all the assistance they can need in their journey of entrepreneurship. As a start-up, we understand the need and requirement of any start-up. We understand the need for proper guidance and mentorship and sometimes finance. We are a platform where you will get all the support and a start-up requires. Through us, you can start your journey and can see your business grow.
