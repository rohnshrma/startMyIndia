1.Business Plan

The business plays an intrinsic role, because it holds the information about the process of work which the company delegated to accomplish, the details about their team members responsibilities that they are required to follow and so on. In basic it holds the information completely about the background of the company such as how it got started , who are all the founders of the company and so on. It also holds the information about the dealers and partners of the company. The business plan holds the details about the market strategies, their competitors and the place they established or try to establish industry. They also hold the services and products that the company is involved in. It contains information about the financial status of the company and also the future requirement to meet the goals. Eventually they also contain the way to get the required funds.

2.Capital

Capital is indeed to run a business. Capital is only the form of cash but also in the form of assertions like machinery and so on. It is essential for the growth and maintenance of the business. Some startup companies or small scale business dealers approach some banks or other organizations which would provide the particular fund for their investment in business with a certain set of rules which is commonly called as loans where in return they need to pay the amount with debits within an allocated period of time. In another way they might get capitals from others in return to get share in the company ownership or profits. Some capitals may have the capability to earn by services like any software, machinery, etc., which used to earn in return of service to other companies when they required it. They are different types of capitals such as capital gain, capital loss, working capital, growing capital and so on.

3.Legal Business Structure.

Once we start the business they need to focus more on choosing the right business structure. It varies based on the type of the business at a company and also the size of the organization whether it is owned by a single person or group of members. The business structure dictates the business owners responsibilities and financial management and the required paperwork for the legal registration of the company. It also contains the tax payment regulation responsibilities, assert production and management and also the potential personnel responsibilities like insurance, legal defense and so on. They are some of the business structures such as sole traders, partnership, company, trust, co-operative, incorporate association and so on. It holds the policy of the company in fields such as who holds the profit and the legal authority for any approval of new techniques, tax regulation periods and methods, recruiting of employees for the company and so on.

4.Employee

Despite the type of business it is vital to choose the employee to fit in your jobs for the augment in their capability of production. Each company has different ways to recruit the employees for their company and also they usually have certain criteria which each employee needs to fulfil in order to get appointed. Some may recruit the employee based on the skill set they required for the upcoming project work which might be required to complete within the deadline. Each company has certain employee policies and rules for welfare of both company and employee. Government has taken necessary steps for employee protection such as work hours, payments and so on. In some companies they provide loans for their company staff and some health insurance policy for employees and their families. They need to maintain some proper paperwork about all these details about their employees in a confidential way.

5.Business License

The business license got varied based on the type of business. The establishment of business needs to be authorized by the government organization and they to satisfy the required statements and follow the allocated rules. The government authority checks the business which you need to start whether it is legal to run. Some types of business like import and export have certain set of rules to be followed in order to avoid any activities which forbid the law or any beach. If the company fails to follow the rules the licence might got cancelled and they aren't able to run the business legally. They check documentation in all perspectives starting from the type of business to the employee regulations. They also go through the facilities in the work space and determine whether it is safe for the workers with proper payment for working hours and even the work time periods. They also go through whether they involved in any child labour and so on.

6.Prototype

Prototype is used to describe the way the product works by using models. The process of creating a prototype seems more useful to demonstrate the flow of the work to their co-workers so that they can get a better understanding about the project. The prototype is mostly used to present the business idea or invention to the investor or their new clients in order to allow them to understand their work and promote them for the investment of funds for their idea. It helps to explain the materials or ingredients required for the work and to enhance the efficiency. It also helps to know about the work on a small scale before investing in a larger production rate in return it mitigates the risk of consequences. In some organizations they have prototype methods to achieve their goals within a short span of time, by this way it encourages the co-workers to understand the situation and take it seriously to reach success.
